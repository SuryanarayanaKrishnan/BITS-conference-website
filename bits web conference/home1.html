2022 International Conference on Advances in Computing, Intelligent Techniques and Networks (CITN 2022)









About BITS:



BITS Pilani, Dubai Campus is the branch campus of the internationally reputed Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani (BITS Pilani), India. It is one of the largest Institutions in the region ranked 5 stars by KHDA in 2020/21 and it is exclusively dedicated to engineering education located at the Dubai International Academic City. BITS Pilani, Dubai Campus offers B.E., M.E., M.B.A. and Ph.D. programmes in various engineering and allied disciplines, wherein nearly 1500 students are admitted from over 20 countries world-wide.

About Conference:



CITN 2022 is the first international conference which is aimed to address the recent developments in the field of computing, intelligence techniques and networks over the globe. The theme of the event is “Seamless Computing for Next Generation”. The aim of conference is to bring together the academia, industry, and forums working in areas of computing, intelligent techniques and networks. The conference will help to promote and explore innovative and ambitious ideas, trends and future challenges towards seamless computing. It comprises issues in current computing systems and intelligent techniques to enhance the ways of computing and data processing and applications of intelligent techniques. The conference will also focus on issues in different networks such as security, network data processing, network without borders, device heterogeneity and different advances in network related to Internet of Things, software defined networks, cloud computing, intelligent networks etc.
